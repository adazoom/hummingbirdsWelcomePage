About Hummingbirds.io

Hummingbirds.io is a web platform that allows users to share and find ideas for programming projectsm, whether it's a hackathon hack or an idea that you want to pursue outside of school, here you will find people to connect with and jump start your project as a team.

This web platform is a part of cs326 class and is licensed under University of Massachusets Amherst.

Our site is currently under development and here you can find the progress of our work as we move along to the project completion
